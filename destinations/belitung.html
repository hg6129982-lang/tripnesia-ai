Belitung Travel Guide

Belitung is one of Indonesia's hidden gems, famous for white-sand beaches, granite rock formations and island hopping adventures.

Top Attractions

  • Tanjung Tinggi Beach
  • Lengkuas Island
  • Kaolin Lake
  • Belitung Mangrove Tour
  • Tanjung Kelayang

Best Time to Visit

March to October offers the best island-hopping conditions.
